Our vision

. The programme’s long-term aspiration is to not only improve the lives of the children enrolled in this project but to find ways of integrating programme components into usual ways of health care delivery. The CDiC project provides access to care for children with diabetes including free of cost human insulin from Novo Nordisk and glucometers, glucose strips, lancets from Roche Diabetes.

Enhancing healthcare by facilitating awareness
empower stakeholders with knowledge about the state of diabetes care
